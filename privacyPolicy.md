# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** June 30, 2026

Your privacy is simple to us: **We do not collect, store, or share any of your personal data.**

Our app does not require a user account, login, or any personal information to function. We do not collect your name, email address, device identifiers, or usage statistics.

We operate entirely client-side. We do not own, rent, or maintain any external servers to track your activity or store your information.

We are simply an interactive app designed for entertainment. Our sole purpose is to display a specifically curated set of card images to users so you can play a fun game. 

## 4. Third-Party Links & Content
The card images displayed are curated for gameplay purposes. The app does not integrate third-party trackers, analytics frameworks, or advertising networks.

## 5. Changes to This Policy
Because we do not collect or track any user information, we have no way to contact you regarding updates. Any changes to this policy will be posted directly on this page.
